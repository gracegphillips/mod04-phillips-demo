Basic Animal Care Information

Grooming

Grooming your animals can provide many benefits. Some of the benefits include improving appearance and the opportunity for bonding between the owner and the animal.

Exercise

It is important to exercise your animals for many reasons. Exercising your animal can stimulate their mind while keeping them healthy.

Housing

We understand here, at Grace's Animal Services, that animals need a stable housing environment. It is important to us to keep your dog feeling at home while they board with us. Having a stable housing environment for your animal keeps them stress-free and less anxious.
